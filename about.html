About Us

Where refined
design meets
genuine warmth.

A 4-star boutique hotel in the heart of Vlorë, Albania. 500 metres from the beach. Spa, sauna, restaurant, bar — and a team that remembers your name.

Our Philosophy

Boutique by choice.
Exceptional
by design.

Chic Boutique Hotel & Spa was built for guests who expect more — not in volume, but in quality. Every room is designed with care: air conditioning, flat-screen TV, minibar, private bathroom with quality toiletries. Not a single detail is an afterthought.

Our spa and sauna exist because we believe a hotel should be a place of genuine restoration, not merely accommodation. Our restaurant terrace faces the city and serves seasonal Albanian ingredients prepared with honest skill.

We are 4 stars. We aim to feel like considerably more.

What We Stand For

Built on three beliefs.

I — Quality

No details skipped.

Every room at Chic is equipped with air conditioning, flat-screen TV, minibar, free WiFi, private bathroom, quality toiletries and daily housekeeping. When we say 4 stars, we mean all of it — not the parts that are convenient to deliver.

II — Hospitality

Albanian warmth.
International standards.

Our front desk operates 24 hours. Our staff speak multiple languages. But more than that — they listen. Restaurant recommendation? Transfer coordination? A local secret? We are here for all of it, without being asked twice.

III — Wellbeing

Rest is part
of the experience.

Our spa and sauna are not an add-on — they are central to what Chic is. Whether you arrive for a business trip or a beach holiday, we believe your body deserves to be taken care of. Soundproof walls, comfortable beds, blackout curtains: sleep is taken seriously here.
