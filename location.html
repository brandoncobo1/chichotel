What's Nearby

Vlorë beyond the hotel.

🏖️

Beaches & Riviera

Vlorë is the northern gateway to the Albanian Riviera. Uji i Ftohtë, Palasa, Drymades, Himara — some of the cleanest water in the Mediterranean — all reachable within an hour. Vlorë Beach itself is a 6-minute walk from reception.

🏛️

History & Culture

Albania declared independence in Vlorë in 1912. The Independence Museum, Muradi Mosque (1542), and the ruins of Apollonia — one of antiquity's most important Greek cities — are all within striking distance. History here is worn lightly but felt deeply.

🍷

Food, Wine & City Life

The Lungomare promenade is Vlorë at its most alive — restaurants, bars, and café terraces running along the seafront. Our team will point you to the places worth going: fresh seafood, local raki, and the informal warmth that defines Albanian evenings.

Getting Here

How to reach us.

By Car

From Tirana: take the SH4 south via Fier (approx. 2h 30). From the Kakavia border (Greece): 1h 45 north via Gjirokastër. Free private parking is available on-site for all guests — no charge, no prior booking needed.

By Bus / Furgon

Direct connections from Tirana's Kombinat terminal run several times daily (approx. 3h). From Greece, cross at Kakavia and connect to Vlorë. We can arrange a complimentary taxi from the bus station — just let us know your arrival time.

Via Corfu

A favourite route from Western Europe: fly to Corfu (CFU), take the short ferry to Sarandë (35 min), then drive or transfer north to Vlorë (1h 30). Scenic, efficient, and a great start to an Albanian Riviera itinerary. Ask us about private transfers.
