Deluxe Triple with Balcony

The Deluxe Triple goes a step further — more space, a minibar worth opening, a tea and coffee maker so mornings start in the room, and two well-configured beds with bedding that makes you sleep longer than planned. The private balcony faces Vlorë. The bathroom is fully equipped and entirely your own.

This is the room for the guest who has stayed in boutique hotels before and knows what separates a good room from a great one. Every element here has been chosen with the belief that small upgrades compound into a meaningfully better experience.

All rooms at Chic include free high-speed Wi-Fi, daily housekeeping, air conditioning, flat-screen TV, private bathroom with quality toiletries, and access to our on-site spa and sauna. Breakfast is available each morning on our restaurant terrace.
